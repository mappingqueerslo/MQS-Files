GALA Pride & Diversity Center

 1060 Palm St, San Luis Obispo, CA 93401
GALA is dedicated to intersectional justice- supporting not only the rights of the LGBTQ+ community but also those of all marginalized groups. Photo from GALA website.
The exterior of the GALA building is bright and inviting, the steps decorated with the colors of the LGBTQ+ pride flag. Photo from Karen Garcia for New Times SLO.

The GALA Center is both an organization and a physical building that both work to support members of the LGBTQ+ community throughout SLO City and County. The center is located at 1060 Palm Street in downtown SLO and serves as a hub for many queer activities as well as a drop-in center for anyone looking for a safe place to be and someone to talk to. They also specialize in community services like housing and healthcare, and have many other resources as well. The organization has existed since 1997, and have been at their location since 2013.
