Skipper's Brew Coffee House - SLO

 1242 Monterey St STE 110, San Luis Obispo, CA 93401
The exterior of Skipper's Brew is decorated with pride flags year-round. Photo by Sydney Sherman for Mustang News.
The owners of Skipper's Brew outside the cafe's San Luis Obispo location. Photo from Skipper's Brew website.

Skipper's Brew is a female queer-owned and queer-friendly coffee shop with locations in downtown SLO and Morro Bay. The cafe is a comfortable place for people to meet up casually and also hosts events for the LGBTQ+ community like drag shows and pride celebrations.

The owners of Skipper's Brew are planning on opening The Platypus - a new cafe that also serves alcohol for those 21+ on Broad Street in SLO. You can read more about its upcoming opening in an article from The SLO Tribune.

You can learn more about Skipper's Brew at their website!
