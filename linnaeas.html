Linnaea's Cafe

 1110 Garden St, San Luis Obispo, CA 93401
This watercolor painting of the front of Linnaea's shows the cafe's affinity and support for the arts.
The back of Linnaea's is a well-maintained garden with plenty of seating for musical performances or just grabbing some breakfast.

Linnaea's is a cafe in downtown SLO at 1100 Garden Street. It has historically been a meeting place for members of the LGBTQ+ community since its opening in 1984. The cafe is known for hosting local artists from the queer community at art shows and musical performances as well as being a safe and welcoming space to unwind, socialize, and enjoy good food and coffee.

Article from November 20, 1997 edition of Mustang Daily showcasing gay community spaces in San Luis Obispo, including Linnaea's.

Linnaea's is big on the local art scene, and thus hosts many different events showcasing Central Coast artists. One event showcasing queer artists titled “No More Rainbows” was held in June 2021. You can read more about the event and its artists at Mustang News' article featuring it.
